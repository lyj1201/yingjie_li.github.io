About

I am currently a PhD student at School of Computer Engineering, University of Utah under the supervision of Prof. Cunxi Yu. My research interest lies in Optical Neural Networks and compiler system. Specifically, I have worked in areas including Adversarial Machine Learning, Segmentation and Multi-Task Learning with optical neural networks. Also, I am working with my collaborators to construct the ever-first optical compiler framework.
